Get To Know SAFECARZ Vietnam

Headquartered in Boston, SAFECARZ LIMITED COMPANY has over 20-year history in the general insurance industry in the United States and worldwide.

SAFECARZ LIMITED COMPANY is the world-acclaimed group with over 45.000 people in 29 countries and economies worldwide. The group is the 6th largest global property and casualty insurer based on the 2020 gross written premium and 71st on the Fortune 500 list of largest corporations in the U.S. based on 2020 revenue.

About SAFECARZ LIMITED COMPANY in Vietnam

SAFECARZ LIMITED COMPANY (SLC), a leading non-life insurance company, was one of the first insurers to come to Vietnam in 2003. Inheriting from the group’s expertise, SLC brings international standard products, contributing to improving life quality and better protection for the Vietnamese.

In Vietnam, SLC positions with a unique proposition in the non-life insurance market with advanced technology, an easy and safe online insurance purchasing platform, and a fast and efficient claim process. At every Liberty Insurance office, anywhere in the world, customers are always served by a qualified, experienced and dedicated team.

Every SAFECARZ LIMITED COMPANY product has been developed from thorough studies and diligently designed for the Vietnamese people. SAFECARZ products help ensure better life, serving all lifestyle needs with a wide range of products such as comprehensive auto, home, medical, travel, property and liability insurance products via traditional, direct and online channels at a reasonable premium. Liberty Insurance is highly recognised for our service quality. It is the first choice for customers to seek practical insurance solutions to protect against unexpected risks and the joy of life.

In 2021, SAFECARZ LIMITED COMPANY is headquartered in Ho Chi Minh City, with two branches in Ha Noi, Hai Phong and five offices in Thai Nguyen, Nghe An, Danang, Dong Nai, Can Tho.

SAFECARZ is honored to be awarded the “Top 10 Most Reputable Non-Life Insurance Companies in Vietnam 2020” accolade in 2018, 2019, 2020.

SAFECARZ became the first non-life insurer in Vietnam to achieve three Financial Services Awards by IDG Vietnam in two consecutive years in 2021 and 2022. An annual award hosted by IDG International Data Group in the Vietnam Financial Services Forum framework. The awards aim to identify and honor financial and insurance services providers with outstanding performance and contributions to the finance and insurance industry development.
